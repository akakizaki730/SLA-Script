Replacing the build chamber external frame gasket (Fuse 1)

Over time, the black build chamber external frame on the top of the build chamber may degrade. If the gasket exhibits cracks or tears, it should be replaced. Debris from the gasket could lead to powder contamination and failed prints, or a worn gasket could allow powder to reach the build chamber’s internals and cause a malfunction.

Use this guide to replace the build chamber’s build chamber external frame.

Required supplies:

  • Replacement build chamber external frame (provided by Formlabs Support)
  • 2 mm hex driver
  • Paper towels

Estimated time: 10–15 minutes

Preparing the workspace

OVERVIEW: Prepare the build chamber and a dust-free workspace for replacing the build chamber external frame

STEP 1: EMPTY THE BUILD CHAMBER

If the build chamber has recently been used for a print and contains a powder cake, empty the build chamber using a Fuse Sift.

STEP 2: VACUUM THE BUILD CHAMBER

Use the vacuum attached to the Fuse Sift to clean the build chamber. If the build chamber is inserted into the Fuse Sift, push the Vac button on the control panel or tap the vacuum hose icon on the touchscreen to turn the vacuum on. Vacuum the print bed and the top of the build chamber to remove any loose powder and prevent contamination of the lead screw grease or your workspace. If the build chamber is not currently inserted into the Fuse Sift, remove the vacuum hose from the port on the side of the Fuse Sift so that it can reach the build chamber, then turn on the vacuum.

STEP 3: PREPARE THE WORKSPACE

Clear a workspace around your build chamber. Ensure that the workspace is well-ventilated and free of dust.

Removing the build chamber external frame

OVERVIEW: Remove the build chamber’s top cover to access and remove the build chamber external frame.

STEP 1: REMOVE THE TOP COVER SCREWS

 

Using a 2 mm hex driver, loosen and remove the 10 M3 × 5 mm screws securing the build chamber’s top cover to the build chamber’s body. Set the screws aside.

STEP 2: REMOVE THE TOP COVER

Remove the top cover and build chamber external frame from the build chamber.

STEP 3: WIPE THE ORANGE SEAL

Use a clean paper towel to clean any powder off of the orange seal below the top cover.

STEP 4: SEPARATE THE EXTERNAL FRAME GASKET FROM THE TOP COVER

Place the top cover face-down. Peel the build chamber external frame away from the top cover.

Installing the new build chamber external frame

OVERVIEW: Install the replacement build chamber external frame and reinstall the top cover.

STEP 1: ORIENT THE TOP COVER AND EXTERNAL FRAME GASKET

Place the top cover face-up, so that the hot surface hazard symbol is visible and facing you. The build chamber external frame has two flanges that sandwich the inner lip of the top cover; position the gasket with the wider flange on the bottom. Orient the new build chamber external frame with the notch facing the back of the top cover (i.e., away from the hot surface hazard symbol).

STEP 2: INSTALL THE REPLACEMENT EXTERNAL FRAME GASKET

Tuck the wider flange of the build chamber external frame under the inner lip of the top cover. Leave the narrower flange on top of the top cover such that the build chamber external frame sandwiches the inner lip of the top cover. Turn the top cover over and check that the screw holes in the gasket are aligned with the screw holes in the top cover.

STEP 3: REINSTALL AND SECURE THE TOP COVER

Position the top cover and build chamber external frame on top of the build chamber. Ensure that the screw holes in the build chamber external frame are aligned with the holes in the top cover and the standoffs on the top surface of the build chamber and that the gasket is not being pinched. Adjust the assembly as needed. Once all of the screw holes are properly aligned, thread one screw into each of the 10 screw holes. Tighten the screws with a 2 mm hex driver until finger tight.

Finalizing the repair

OVERVIEW: After reinstalling the build chamber’s top cover, perform final checks.

STEP 1: TEST THE BUILD CHAMBER

Insert the build chamber into the Fuse 1 and start a print. Monitor the build chamber to ensure that the print bed moves smoothly and that the printer dispenses powder into the build chamber normally. Contact Formlabs Support or your certified service provider to share the results of the procedure.
