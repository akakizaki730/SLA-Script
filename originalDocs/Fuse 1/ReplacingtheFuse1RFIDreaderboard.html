Replacing the Fuse 1 RFID reader board

Each package of Formlabs powder includes an ID card. This card contains powder credits which must be loaded onto the printer via the RFID reader at the top of the machine to authorize and track the amount of powder that is available. A malfunctioning RFID reader board will prevent the printer from reading from or writing to the powder ID card properly.

Use this guide to replace the Fuse 1 RFID reader board.

Required Supplies:

  • Replacement RFID reader board (provided by Formlabs Support or your certified service provider)
  • 2.5 mm hex driver (provided by Formlabs Support or your certified service provider)
  • 3 mm hex driver (provided by Formlabs Support or your certified service provider)
  • T10 Torx driver (provided by Formlabs Support or your certified service provider)
  • Piece of cardboard or cardstock
  • Nitrile gloves

Estimated time: 30-45 minutes

DANGER: Read and follow all safety instructions and warnings in the operator manual. Replacement with incorrect parts can lead to burns, shock, fire, or explosion. Dispose of all parts in accordance with local regulations.

Preparing the workspace

OVERVIEW: Prepare the Fuse 1 and a dust-free workspace for replacing the RFID reader board.

NOTICE: The RFID reader board is a sensitive electronic component. Do not remove the RFID reader board from its packaging until instructed.

STEP 1: PREPARE THE WORKSPACE

Clear a workspace around your printer. Ensure that the workspace is well-ventilated and free of dust.

STEP 2: UNPLUG THE PRINTER

Disconnect the power cable before continuing with maintenance. Do not perform maintenance on the printer while it is connected to power. Wait five minutes after disconnecting the printer from power before proceeding.

DANGER: Performing maintenance on the printer while it is plugged in increases the risk of electric shock. Never remove the back panel of the printer while the printer is plugged in. Never reconnect the printer to power while the back panel is uninstalled.

Removing the RFID reader board

OVERVIEW: Unplug, unscrew, and remove the RFID reader board from the printer.

DANGER: The Fuse 1 contains sensitive electronic components. Ground yourself before touching any electronics in the printer by using a grounding strap or touching a grounded piece of metal.
